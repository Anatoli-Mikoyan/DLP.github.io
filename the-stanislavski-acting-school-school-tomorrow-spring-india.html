Tomorrow Spring India

The Stanislavski School of Acting

Amit Jain

Artistic Director of the Indian Masterclass

Bridging Artistic Cultures We are proud to introduce a unique initiative: Tomorrow Spring Stanislavski school of acting, dedicated to training Indian actors in the Stanislavski System under the artistic direction of Amit Jain. This exceptional project aims to merge the cultural richness of Belarus and India, offering top-tier theatrical training.

Amit Jain, a renowned filmmaker and educator, serves as the Artistic Director of the Indian Masterclass in Minsk. With his diverse experience and collaborations with leading figures in theatre and cinema, Amit personally selects the actors and ensures a rigorous and immersive training experience. The courses, taught by Belarusian experts, are translated from Russian to English to ensure full comprehension and seamless cultural integration for Indian participants.

Our program is inspired by the renowned Stanislavski System, providing students with a proven method to develop their craft. Through both theoretical and practical training, actors will learn to explore and refine their acting skills while benefiting from a unique intercultural perspective.

Join us in Minsk for an enriching experience that will open new artistic and professional pathways, and help forge lasting connections between the theatrical cultures of Belarus and India.
